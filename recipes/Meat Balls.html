Meat Balls

Description

Meatballs are small, round balls made from ground meat, usually beef, pork, or a mix of both.
They often include breadcrumbs, eggs, and seasonings like garlic, onion, and herbs.
Meatballs are typically cooked by frying, baking, or simmering in a sauce.
They can be served with pasta, in a sandwich, or on their own as an appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp parsley
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp thyme

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  3. Form into 1 inch balls and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes.
