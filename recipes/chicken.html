Chicken Stew

Description

Chicken stew is a simple and hearty dish.
It includes chicken pieces, onions and tomatoes cooked together it makes a flavorful broth, the stew becomes rich and tasty.
It's perfect for a warm, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tea spoons oil
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 1 tea spoon pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry the chicken pieces until golden brown.
  2. Add the onions and fry until soft.
  3. Add the tomatoes and fry until soft.
  4. Add the water, salt and pepper and bring to a boil.
  5. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
