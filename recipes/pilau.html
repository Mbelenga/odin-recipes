Pilau

Description

Pilau is a traditional dish enjoyed in many cultures, known for its fragrant aroma and flavorful spices.
This dish is a perfect blend of rice, meat, and spices, creating a rich and satisfying meal that is both comforting and delicious.

The recipe involves cooking rice with a variety of spices such as cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves, along with tender pieces of meat and sometimes vegetables.
The result is a beautifully spiced and hearty dish that can be enjoyed on its own or with a side of salad or chicken stew.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup of water
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil the meat
  2. Chop the onions, tomatoes and crush the garlic
  3. Put the meat, onions, tomatoes and garlic in a pot
  4. Put the spices
  5. Put the rice
  6. Put the water
  7. Let it simmer down until it has cooked
